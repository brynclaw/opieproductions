What SEO does for a business site

SEO helps search engines understand your pages so they can match you with the right searches. It also helps real people. A clear site structure, fast pages, and helpful content brings more leads and more customers.

Common signs you need SEO work:

Your site ranks for your business name, but not for your services

Traffic looks flat month after month

Pages load slow on mobile

Old pages show up in Google, or the wrong page ranks

Your SEO plan starts with search terms that fit your services and your customers' intent. A dentist and a hotel do not need the same page plan.

You do not need to rank for every term, but you do need to rank for the terms that lead to real customers.

On-page SEO basics that move the needle

These items should live on each page. They help Google and visitors read the page the same way.

Focus on:

One clear topic per page, stated in the H1

Page titles that match the search term and the service

Headings that break content into short, scannable blocks

Internal links that point to related services and next steps

Small changes here can lift rankings faster than a full redesign.
